Description

The original designers of the 990 were tasked with creating the best running shoe on the market. The finished product more than lived up to his expectations. When the 990 was first launched in 1982, it was an elegant, understated gray and cost a three-digit amount at the time. For avid runners and trendsetters alike, the 990 was a sign of quality and great taste. Since '82 the design has been revised and there have been more color options, but the aura of the 990's rising status symbol has remained the same. Put simply - the 990 is a shoe so good we never stopped making it. The 990v3 features a premium upper and ENCAP midsole.

New Balance MADE in US footwear contains 70% or more domestic value added. MADE accounts for a limited portion of our US sales.
